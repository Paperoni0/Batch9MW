Welcome to the official digital archive of SMA Maitreyawira Palembang Batch 9.

This website preserves the creative works, projects, and memories created by our graduating class, ensuring they remain accessible for years to come.

About Batch 9

Built by Students, for Students

Batch9MW is a collaborative initiative created by students of SMA Maitreyawira Palembang Batch 9. Originally developed as a school project, it has grown into a digital archive dedicated to preserving our creative works and showcasing the achievements of our batch.

Every poem, project, and future archive tells part of our shared journey.

Featured Project

Poetry Collection

The Poetry Collection is the first archived project of Batch9MW. It features original poems written by students across all five classes, preserving each work as a lasting memory of our school years.

Our Mission

We believe creative works deserve to be preserved.

Batch9MW exists to document the ideas, creativity, and memories of Batch 9, creating a digital archive that future students, teachers, and alumni can revisit with pride and nostalgia.
